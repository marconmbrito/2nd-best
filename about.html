I'm Marco, a self-taught front-end developer. I am also a Business Management student and passionate about the work I do. Brazilian from the beautiful Rio de Janeiro, I moved to Stockholm on June/2017.

I started my developer journey on may/2017. Having an entrepreneurial vein, I was looking for a growing market/career paths to follow in my life. I knew I wanted to make a difference, even though I was not sure on how. That was until I found coding.

I learned English in 1,5 year without ever studying in school. I learned how to play guitar by myself in 2 months. Spanish, bartending flair, cooking and other less-important skills are some of the things I hide under my tool-belt.

Right now, more than learning any language, I am focusing on my strength on learning to be a developer. How to think like a dev, how to Google like a dev, and most important of all, how to solve problems like a dev.
